Unordered List (Ingredients for Pancakes)

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Ordered List (Cooking Instructions)

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the dry ingredients
  2. In another bowl, beat the egg, then add in milk and melted butter
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until combined
  4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat
  5. Pour the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake
  6. Cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, then flip and cook until browned
